About Us

We’re Jon and Ryan, creators who took to Kickstarter to realize products that we designed and were ready to share with the world. Through this process, over the last 10 years, we’ve gained a lot of experience in how to setup a project for success. We want to help others get their products off the ground with this level of insight and consideration before launch. We learned from our failures so you don’t have to. Let’s help make your launch a success!




Our Mission

We started this work to pay it forward. We’ve been fortunate to have repeat successes in launching campaigns, designing well received products, and building communities that support them. We provide a range of paid and pro bono services to help creators bring their products to life. We know that not everyone can afford the assistance to launch their great ideas, so we are donating 20% of our time to help fellow creators.
